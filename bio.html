About Me

I like to see, do, and learn new stuff. Whether it's backpacking and volunteering around South America, living in China for a year to learn Chinese, or moving to Thailand to study Muay Thai for six months, I'm getting out of my comfort zone.

When I'm not making cool stuff for the internet or moving to different continents, you can probably find me hanging out with my family's dogs, talking about Gator football, or trying to recreate some exotic dish I had on a street corner for friends and family (usually unsuccessfully).
